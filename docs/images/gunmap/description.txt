barrel - The metal tube through which a projectile or shot charge is fired

buttstock - The stock of a firearm in the rear of the breech mechanism
 
front sight - It assist in visually aligning ranged weapons

grip - A handle that assists users at vertical aiming

handguard - Barrel shroud specifically designed to allow the user to grip the front of the gun

lower receiver - It normally normally house the trigger, trigger group, and magazine
 
magazine - An ammunition storage and feeding device for a repeating firearm, either integral within the gun (internal/fixed magazine) or externally attached (detachable magazine).

upper receiver -It normally houses the barrel, breach, firing chamber, and bolt or bolt carrier grouping
